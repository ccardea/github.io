## Old School Yoga

### History
This was not my first web development project, but marks my re-entry after an absence of several years. The site reflects my long-time interest in the subject matter and was conceived as a public service project intended for newcomers to Yoga philosophy. The site was built using the Jekyll framework. 

### Current Status
The site is stable and updated infrequently.  
[old-school-yoga.com](https://old-school-yoga.com)  
[old-school-yoga.org](https://old-school-yoga.org)

### Plans For The Future

#### Migration to Astro 
I've decided to migrate the site to Astro. This will include a re-design of the site and a review and update of site content.

#### Domain Reorganization 
Currently, old-school-yoga .com and .org domains both point to the same site. The .org domain will be converted to an organization site focused on member management and communication, and members-only content.

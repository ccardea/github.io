## BHSharps
[bhsharps.com](https://bhsharps.com)

### History
This was my first javascript based site. It was originally developed using plain HTML, CSS, and Javascript, and implemented several custom elements. The site worked well and was more highly developed than it is at present, but before the first release, I decided to simplify and convert it to Astro.

### Current Status
The site is relatively stable, with updates to content and internals as needed. I have started work on an authentication worker that will enable customer and admin access to protected content. The system is loosely based on oidc and is intended to service multiple sites.

### Plans For the Future
There is a lot of room for improvement here, driven by the needs of the business. Areas for development include both customer facing and internal projects.
